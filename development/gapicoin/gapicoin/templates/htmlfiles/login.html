4. COOKIES AND OTHER DATA COLLECTION TECHNOLOGY

Cookies are small text files that are sent to or accessed from your web browser or computer hard drive. A cookie usually contains a unique number or similar identifier name for the domain (Internet location) where the cookie was created, the ”lifetime son of the cookie (that is, when it ended), and a randomly generated number. The cookie may also contain information about your computer, such as user settings, browsing history, and activities performed while using Services. A web beacon (also known as a pixel tag or net GIF) is a piece of computer code that allows us to monitor user activity and website traffic.
